Official White Elephant Rules (2025)

Whether you call it White Elephant, Yankee Swap, or Dirty Santa, the core rules are the same. It's the ultimate holiday icebreaker.

  1. Bring a Gift: Everyone brings one wrapped gift. (Typical budget: $20-$25).
  2. Draw Numbers: Use our generator to assign a random order.
  3. Player #1: Picks and unwraps a gift.
  4. Next Players: Can choose to unwrap a NEW gift or STEAL an opened gift.
  5. Steal Limit: A gift can usually be stolen 3 times before it is "frozen".
  6. The End: Game ends when the last gift is opened. Player #1 often gets to swap at the end!
