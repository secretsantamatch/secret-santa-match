Secret Santa Rules & Etiquette (2025 Guide)

The Official Rules

  1. Set a Budget: Common limits are $20, $25, or $50. Ensure everyone knows this upfront.
  2. Draw Names Randomly: Use our generator to ensure fairness and anonymity.
  3. No Telling: The identity of the giver must remain a secret until the gift is opened.
  4. Wishlists Help: Encourage participants to fill out their profile so they receive something they actually want.

Popular Variations

  • White Elephant / Yankee Swap: Instead of assigned names, everyone brings a generic gift and players can "steal" from each other. Use our White Elephant Tool for this!
  • Secret Santa Conspiracy: Everyone teams up to buy one huge gift for one person.
  • Thrift Store Challenge: All gifts must be second-hand or upcycled.

Our algorithm handles odd numbers automatically. It ensures that everyone gives one gift and receives one gift, creating a perfect loop (A gives to B, B gives to C, C gives to A).
