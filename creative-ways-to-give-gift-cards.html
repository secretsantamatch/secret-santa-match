7 The Scratch-Off Reveal Medium$5-10

Create a DIY scratch-off card revealing the retailer, amount, or redemption code.

What You Need:

  • Cardstock
  • Clear packing tape
  • Dish soap + acrylic paint (2:1 ratio)
  • Sponge brush

How to: Write the gift card details on cardstock, cover with packing tape, paint over with the soap/paint mixture (2-3 coats), let dry. They scratch off to reveal the surprise!

Why it works: The tactile experience of scratching off makes it feel like a lottery ticket or game‚Äîway more fun than just opening an envelope.

8 The "Choose Your Adventure" Multi-Card Medium$15-25

Give them 3-4 smaller gift cards to different stores instead of one large one. Present them as options for different moods or needs.

Example setup ($100 total):

  • $40 Target card: "For when you need the essentials"
  • $30 Starbucks card: "For when you need a pick-me-up"
  • $30 AMC card: "For when you need an escape"
Why it works: Multiple cards feel more generous than one, plus it shows you thought about different aspects of their life. The labels make it personal and fun.
