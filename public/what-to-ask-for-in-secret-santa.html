How to Create Your Perfect Secret Santa Wish List

The Three-Item Rule

To give your Secret Santa flexibility while ensuring you get something you want, provide three different options at varying price points:

  • Budget pick: Something under $15.
  • Mid-range option: An item in the $15-$25 range.
  • Splurge item: Something from $25-$35 (if the budget allows).

Be Specific But Not Too Specific

  • Too vague: "Something for my kitchen"
  • Just right: "A silicone spatula set, measuring cups, or kitchen towels"
  • Too specific: "Apple AirPods Pro 2nd Generation in white only"
  • Just right: "Wireless earbuds (any brand) or headphone accessories"

Things to Avoid Asking For

  • Items way over the budget
  • Anything too personal (e.g., underwear)
  • Highly controversial items
  • Live animals or plants (unless pre-approved)
  • Gag gifts only (unless that's the theme)

What to Say When Asked About Your Wish List

  • Casual Conversation Starters: "I've been eyeing...", "I could really use...", "I've heard great things about..."
  • If You're Struggling: "I'm pretty easy! I love anything cozy, coffee-related, or for my home office. Gift cards are always a great option, too!"
  • If "I Really Don't Need Anything": Suggest consumables (food, candles), experiences (movie tickets), or a donation to your favorite charity.
  • If You're Worried About Seeming Greedy: Frame it as helping them! "To make shopping easier, here are a few things I've had my eye on..."
