Pro-Tips for Perfect Printing

  • Use Cardstock: For a sturdy, high-quality feel, print your tags on white cardstock (80 lb or heavier is ideal).
  • Try Sticker Paper: For an even easier option, print the tags on full-sheet sticker paper. Just print, cut, peel, and stick!
  • Printer Settings: Make sure your printer is set to "Best" or "High Quality" and that you're printing at 100% scale (not "Fit to Page").
  • Finishing Touches: Use a hole punch for the ribbon hole and a paper cutter for perfectly straight lines.
