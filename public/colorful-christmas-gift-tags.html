About These Colorful Tags

This set is designed for a fun, contemporary Christmas look. With bold typography and a non-traditional holiday color palette, these tags are perfect for pairing with solid-colored or simple patterned wrapping paper to create a stunning, modern effect.

Printing Tips

  • Paper: A bright white, heavy cardstock (80lb or higher) is essential to make these colors look their best.
  • Printer Settings: Print at 100% scale and use your printer's best quality color setting.
  • Finishing: Pair with a contrasting ribbon or twine to make the colors stand out even more. A bright pink or turquoise ribbon would look amazing!
