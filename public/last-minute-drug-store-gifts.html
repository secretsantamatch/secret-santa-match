15 Surprisingly Great Last-Minute Gifts From The Drug Store

It happens to the best of us: a party invitation slips your mind, or you realize you forgot someone on your list. Before you panic, take a deep breath and head to your nearest drug store. It’s a goldmine of thoughtful, fun, and genuinely great gifts if you know where to look.

Here are 15 fantastic last-minute gift ideas you can grab on your way to the party.

For the Self-Care Enthusiast

  1. A Curated Spa Basket: Grab a small basket and fill it with face masks, a nice body lotion, a bath bomb, and some Epsom salts. It’s a classic for a reason!
  2. High-Quality Skincare Set: Many drug stores carry popular and effective skincare brands like CeraVe, La Roche-Posay, or Neutrogena. A gift set with a cleanser and moisturizer is always a safe bet.
  3. Essential Oil Diffuser & Oils: Check the wellness aisle for a simple, affordable essential oil diffuser. Pair it with a calming lavender or uplifting citrus oil.
  4. Cozy Comfort Kit: Combine a pair of fuzzy socks, a nice lip balm (like Burt's Bees or Aquaphor), and a rich hand cream for a perfect little comfort package.

For the Foodie or Host

  1. Gourmet Chocolate & Coffee: Most drug stores have a surprisingly good selection of high-end chocolate bars (think Lindt or Ghirardelli). Pair one with a bag of quality ground coffee for a delicious duo.
  2. A Bottle of Wine or Craft Beer: If your state's laws permit, the beverage aisle can be your best friend. Many stores stock decent local or popular wine and beer options.
  3. A Collection of Magazines: Pick out 2-3 magazines tailored to their interests—whether it’s cooking, home decor, or cars. Tie them together with a festive ribbon.
  4. Snack Attack Box: Fill a gift bag with an assortment of fun, interesting, or international snacks from the candy and chip aisles. Everyone loves discovering a new favorite treat.

For Anyone on Your List

  1. A Classic Board Game or Deck of Cards: Look in the toy aisle for timeless games like Uno, Jenga, or a nice deck of playing cards. It’s a gift that encourages fun and connection.
  2. A Phone Power Bank: In today’s world, a portable charger is one of the most useful gifts you can give. You'll usually find them in the electronics aisle.
  3. A Nice Water Bottle or Travel Mug: A stylish, reusable water bottle or an insulated travel mug is a practical gift anyone can appreciate.
  4. Gift Cards Galore: The gift card rack is your ultimate fallback. Whether it's for a coffee shop, a restaurant, a streaming service, or a big-box store, it's a gift that always fits.

For a Festive Touch

  1. A Beautiful Scented Candle: From seasonal scents like pine and cinnamon to year-round favorites like vanilla or linen, a good candle makes any space feel cozier.
  2. A Christmas Ornament: During the holidays, you’ll find a selection of festive ornaments. It’s a small, sweet gesture that can be treasured for years.
  3. Lottery Scratch-Off Tickets: Tuck a few scratch-off tickets into a greeting card for a fun, low-cost gift that comes with a thrill of possibility!

The key is presentation! Grab a nice gift bag, some colorful tissue paper, and a thoughtful card—all available in the same store, of course. With a little creativity, no one will ever guess your thoughtful gift was a last-minute find.
