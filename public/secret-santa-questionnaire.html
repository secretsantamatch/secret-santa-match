What’s Inside the Printable

Our free questionnaire is packed with useful prompts. Here's a breakdown:

  • "My Favorites" Section: The heart of the questionnaire. Participants can list their go-to restaurant, favorite store, preferred music artist, or favorite movie.
  • "This or That" Choices: Super helpful for those who aren’t great at filling out open-ended forms. They can simply check a box for things like "Sweet" or "Salty," "Coffee" or "Tea," and "Cook" or "Order In."
  • "I Absolutely Love" & "I Don't Like": Arguably the most important parts! They allow the participant to tell you exactly what they want and what to avoid.
  • "I Really Need" & "I Don't Need": A bonus section perfect for coworkers or close friends to avoid buying something they already have.
  • Allergies: A key safety feature! Participants can list any allergies to make sure no gifts contain anything that could cause a reaction.
