Printing Tips for the Best Results

  • Use Cardstock: These jokes land better on sturdy paper. White cardstock (80-110 lb) is perfect.
  • Make Stickers: Print on full-sheet sticker paper and apply them directly to your gifts.
  • Choose High Quality: In your printer settings, select "High" or "Best" quality to make the colors and text sharp.
