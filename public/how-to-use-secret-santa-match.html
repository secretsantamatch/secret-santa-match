Step 1: Add Participants

This is the starting point. In the "Add Participants" section, simply start typing names.

  • For Wishlists: Click the "Details" button next to a name to add gift ideas, links, or a specific budget for that person.
  • Bulk Add: Have a list of names already? Click "Bulk Add from List," paste your names (one per line), and they'll all be added at once.

Step 2: Set Rules & Details

Scroll down to the "Add Details & Rules" section. This is where you customize the exchange.

  • Event Details: Add a message that everyone will see, like "Gift exchange at the holiday party on Dec 20th. Budget: $25."
  • Exclusions: This is crucial. Don't want couples drawing each other? Add an exclusion rule (e.g., "Sarah can't draw Ben").
  • Assignments (Optional): Need to force a specific match? Use this to set a rule like "Michael must draw Dwight."

Step 3: Style Your Printable Cards

If you're having an in-person party, our printable cards are a game-changer. In the "Style Your Cards" section, you can choose from themes, upload your own background, and customize fonts and colors. You'll see a live preview update on the right as you make changes!

Step 4: Generate the Matches!

Once everything is set up, click the big "üéÅ Generate Matches" button at the bottom. The tool will instantly work its magic, creating random pairings that follow all of your rules.

Step 5: Share the Secrets

You'll be taken to the results page, your organizer dashboard. Here you have two main options for sharing:

  • Digital/Remote Groups: Click "Share Private Links" to get a unique link for each person. Copy and send these links via text, WhatsApp, Slack, etc.
  • In-Person Parties: Click "Download Now" to save and print the beautifully styled individual cards to hand out at your event. You can also download a Master List for your eyes only.

Pro Tips for Organizers

  • Sharing Long Links: The private links are long for privacy reasons. To make them cleaner, use a free service like TinyURL.com to shorten them. Or, hyperlink text like "Click here for your match!" so the recipient never sees the long URL.
  • Use a Questionnaire: For great gift ideas, send out our free printable questionnaire beforehand. You can then copy/paste the answers into the "Details" section.
  • Send a Reminder: A day or two before the exchange, send a group message reminding everyone of the date and budget.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SecretSantaMatch.com really free?

Yes, 100%! All features are completely free. The site is supported by ads, so you never have to pay.

Why are the shareable links so long?

For your privacy! All your event data is stored securely in the link itself, not on our servers. This means we never see or save your private data.

What if I make a mistake?

On the results page, click the 'Make Changes' button. This reloads the generator with all your previous info, so you can easily make edits and regenerate the matches.
