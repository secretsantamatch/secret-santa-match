What Makes a Great Streaming Gift?

The best gifts enhance the experience. Think about it in three categories:

  • Comfort: Things that make their viewing spot cozier (blankets, pillows, trays).
  • Quality: Tech that improves the sound or picture (soundbars, streaming sticks, backlights).
  • Fun: Items that connect to the content they love (cookbooks, games, posters).
