Privacy Policy for SecretSantaMatch.com

Last Updated: September 24, 2024

Welcome to SecretSantaMatch.com ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

Our service is designed for privacy. We do not require user accounts, emails, or any personally identifiable information to generate Secret Santa matches.

The information you provide (participant names, event details, gift ideas, etc.) is processed locally in your browser and then encoded directly into the shareable URLs. **This data is not stored on our servers.**

2. Data Storage and Security

All data related to your Secret Santa exchange is contained within the URL hash. This means the data resides on your device and is only shared when you choose to share the links. We do not have access to this data.

We use local browser storage (localStorage) to temporarily save your list while you are setting up your event. This allows you to refresh the page without losing your work. This data remains on your computer and is not transmitted to us. You can clear this data at any time by using the "Clear" button in the app or by clearing your browser's cache.

3. Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising purposes to support the site and improve our service.

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how our service is used (e.g., which features are popular, how many visitors we get). This helps us make improvements. This service collects anonymous data and does not track any of the personal names or details you enter for your gift exchange.
  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on the site. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, please see Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. Affiliate Disclosure

This site may participate in affiliate marketing programs, such as the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. This means if you click on an affiliate link (for example, in a blog post or recommendation) and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This helps support the free service we provide.

5. Third-Party Links

This site may contain links to other websites (e.g., our blog, social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those other sites.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@secretsantamatch.com.
