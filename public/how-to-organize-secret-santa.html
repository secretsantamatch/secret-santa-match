Don't just say "$20-ish." Say exactly $20. Vague budgets lead to someone spending $50 while another person brings a $10 gift card. Be specific.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything else you're probably wondering

Do people really need to create accounts?

Not with the right tool! The best free Secret Santa generators require zero accounts, zero emails, and zero sign-ups. You just enter names, set preferences, generate matches, and share links. That's it. Anyone asking for an email is either collecting data to sell or planning to spam you with "upgrade" offers.

Can I still do Secret Santa if some people aren't tech-savvy?

Absolutely. The beauty of link-based systems is that clicking a link requires the same skill level as opening a text message. If your aunt can check Facebook, she can click her Secret Santa link. You can also print cards for anyone who prefers paper‚Äîbest of both worlds.

What if someone loses their match link?

As the organizer, you should keep the master list (either downloaded or bookmarked). You can resend someone's specific link anytime. This is why having a master list is crucial‚Äîit's your safety net.

How do exclusions actually work?

You specify who can't be matched together before generating matches. Common examples: married couples, siblings, people in the same department, or anyone who bought for someone last year. The algorithm ensures these pairings never happen.

Is it really free, or is there a catch?

Legitimately free tools make money through optional features or ads, not by charging you. If a tool is free but requires an email, they're selling your data. If it's free with a "pro" version, check what features are actually locked‚Äîmost people never need them.

Can I organize multiple Secret Santa groups?

Yes! Most free tools let you create unlimited events. Just bookmark or save each event's organizer link. Perfect if you're doing separate exchanges for family, work, and friends.

What happens if I need to add someone after matches are made?

Depends on the tool. Some let you regenerate while keeping certain matches intact. Others require starting fresh. This is where having your participant list saved helps‚Äîyou can regenerate in seconds if needed.

Should I tell people the budget before or after they see their match?

BEFORE. Always before. Set the budget when you create the event so it's visible to everyone from the start. Nothing's worse than finding out about a $50 budget after you already bought a $20 gift.
