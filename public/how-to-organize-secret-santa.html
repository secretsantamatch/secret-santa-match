Pro Tips for Success

Small details that make a huge difference:

  • Set a real budget: Don't just say "$20-ish." Say exactly $20.
  • Give enough time: Send out matches at least 2-3 weeks before the exchange.
  • Encourage wishlists: Even vague hints like "Loves coffee" or "Into plants" help immensely.
  • Theme it up: "Ugly sweater gifts" or "handmade only" themes can make it more fun.
