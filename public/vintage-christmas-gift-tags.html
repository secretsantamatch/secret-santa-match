About These Art Deco Tags

Inspired by the opulence and distinctive style of the 1920s, these tags are designed for those who appreciate a touch of luxury. The bold lines, metallic gold accents, and deep colors will make any gift look exceptionally chic.

Printing Tips

  • Paper: Use a bright white, heavy cardstock (80lb or higher) to make the colors and gold accents pop. A paper with a slight sheen could also look stunning.
  • Printer Settings: Print at 100% scale or "Actual Size." Use your printer's highest quality setting for the best results.
  • Finishing Touches: A simple hole punch and some thin, metallic gold ribbon or black satin ribbon would be the perfect complement to these tags.
