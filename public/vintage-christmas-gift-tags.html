Printing Tips for a Perfect Vintage Look

  • Use Cream Cardstock: For an extra authentic vintage feel, print on an off-white or cream-colored cardstock.
  • Choose High Quality: In your printer settings, select "High" or "Best" quality to make the rich colors pop.
  • Add a Twine Tie: Use natural jute twine or a simple red ribbon to attach the tags for a classic finish.
