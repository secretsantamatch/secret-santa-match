About These Snarky Tags

These tags are for those who believe the holidays should be fun, not just formal. With messages that are equal parts hilarious and honest, they're guaranteed to get a laugh. Perfect for White Elephant exchanges or for that one cousin who shares your sense of humor.

Printing Tips

  • Paper: Standard white cardstock works great. For a more rustic look, try printing on kraft paper cardstock.
  • Printer Settings: Print at 100% scale and use a high-quality setting.
  • Finishing: Cut them out, punch a hole, and attach with some simple baker's twine for a classic look.
