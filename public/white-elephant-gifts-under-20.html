Frequently Asked Questions

What is the point of a White Elephant gift exchange?

The point is entertainment! Unlike Secret Santa where gifts are personal, White Elephant gifts are usually funny, weird, or semi-useful items that create a fun game of choosing and stealing gifts among players.

What is a good budget for White Elephant?

A budget of $20 to $25 is the most common and effective range for a White Elephant party. It's high enough for creative and funny gifts but low enough that no one gets too attached if their gift gets stolen.

What's the difference between funny and useless?

A funny gift has entertainment value (like the Screaming Goat). A useless gift is just junk (like a broken toaster). Aim for funny, never useless!
