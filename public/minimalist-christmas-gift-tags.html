About These Minimalist Tags

Less is more. This collection focuses on beautiful fonts and simple, elegant layouts. They are versatile enough to complement any wrapping paper, from simple kraft paper to bold, graphic patterns.

Printing Tips

  • Paper: These look stunning on a high-quality, bright white cardstock. For a different look, try printing on black cardstock with a white toner printer, or on a textured linen paper.
  • Printer Settings: Print at 100% scale and use a high-quality setting to ensure the fine lines of the typography are crisp.
  • Finishing: A simple black or white twine, or a very thin silk ribbon, will complete the minimalist look perfectly.
